Arithmeticker math with dates and times

    Documentation

    Equations are entered in the textarea on the left.

    Results for each line are displayed to the right.

    Operators

    Addition + / plus
    Subtraction - / minus
    Format in / as followed by unit of time

    Placeholders

    Now now
    Today today
    Yesterday yesterday aka today - 1 day
    Tomorrow tomorrow aka today + 1 day

    Units of time

    Second seconds / second / sec
    Minute minutes / minute / min
    Hour hours / hour / hrs / hr
    Day days / day
    Week weeks / week / wks / wk
    Fortnight seconds / second / sec

    Locations in time

    • DD/MM/YYYY
    • HH:MM DD/MM/YYYY
    • HH:MM:SS
